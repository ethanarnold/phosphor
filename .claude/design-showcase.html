Design · v0.1 · 2026-04-21
A design direction for a lab research tool

Ink on paper.
One signal at a time.

Confidence through typography and restraint, not color. Fraunces sets the voice; Inter Tight carries the interface; JetBrains Mono handles data, metadata, and scale. Hairline rules structure the page. Nothing is decorative.
